The shop customer data dataset from Kaggle is a collection of information about customers who shop at a particular store, the dataset contains 2000 records and 8 columns including customer id gender age annual income spending score profession work experience and family size the dataset provides a detailed analysis of the ideal customers of the shop, which can help the business owner better understand their customer base, the information is obtained through membership cards which are used by customers when making purchases at the store, the dataset includes information about the demographic characteristics of customers such as age, gender and family size as well as their spending behaviour as indicated by their spending score. The spending score is assigned by the shop, based on customer behaviour and spending patterns. for this project i used r-studio to analyse and code
